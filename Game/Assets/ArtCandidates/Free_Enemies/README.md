# Free Enemy Candidates

## Free Robot Warfare Pack - Primary Enemy Set

Source: https://mattwalkden.itch.io/free-robot-warfare-pack

- Direct free download under CC0.
- Includes five robot insects with melee and ranged attacks.
- Also includes badlands and city tiles, explosions, muzzle flashes,
  projectiles, debris, props, and UI.
- Credits are appreciated but not required.
- Planned roles: chaser, drone, support enemy, projectiles, effects, and
  additional wasteland environment art.

## Tyst Top Down Robotic Enemies

Source: https://tyst.itch.io/top-down-robotic-enemies

- Zero-cost `Name your own price` download.
- Includes separate melee and ranged enemy animation sets.
- The creator explicitly permits commercial use and says credit is appreciated
  but not required.
- Planned role: player scavenger robot after a cyan recolor and silhouette edit.

## Vircon32 Robot Sprites

Source: https://opengameart.org/content/robot-sprites

- Free under CC-BY 4.0.
- Includes one eight-direction robot and two sizes of cannons on wheels.
- Attribution is required.
- Planned roles: player, ranged enemy, or heavy turret.

## Modular 64x Robots

Source: https://croomfolk.itch.io/modular-64x-robots

- Zero-cost `Name your own price` download.
- CC-BY-SA 4.0 with attribution and ShareAlike requirements.
- Includes 27 interchangeable tracked robot parts aligned to a 64x64 grid.
- Planned roles: drone and support enemy silhouettes.

## Robot's Last Stand

Source: https://ninbax-303.itch.io/robots-last-stand-asset-pack

- Direct free download under CC0.
- Includes one player, two enemies, projectiles, and damage sprites.
- Planned role: backup source for enemy variants and projectiles.
