# Gear Scavenger Room Features

Each room should change how the player moves, aims, or evaluates risk. Room
features are gameplay tools rather than decoration.

| Feature | Behavior | Player decision |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Reinforced Barricade | Blocks movement and projectiles until destroyed | Keep it as cover or spend damage to open a route |
| Unstable Shock Field | Pulses every 1.65 seconds for 16 enemy damage and 7 player damage | Lure awakened machines into it without being caught |
| Coolant Recovery Zone | Rapidly vents player heat and slows enemies to 52% speed | Hold the zone for sustained fire or retreat through it |
| Recovered Defense Turret | Fires at awakened enemies within 6.2 units | Fight near the turret to gain extra pressure |
| Skill Core | Grants a unique persistent or temporary upgrade | Explore each combat room before moving on |

## Skill Cores

| Core | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| Kinetic Overdrive | For 24 seconds, movement speed increases by 25% and firing delay is reduced by 28% |
| Coolant Matrix | Permanently reduces heat generated by shots by 22% and immediately vents all heat |
| Nanite Shell | Permanently adds 25 maximum armor and repairs 45 armor |
| Salvage Magnet | Permanently increases scrap pickup radius and grants 8 scrap |

## Room Identities

- **Scrap Yard:** Open fighting space with an unstable shock field and a
  reinforced barricade. The player can kite enemies through the pulse.
- **Assembly Maze:** Dense cover, destructible objects, and a central shock
  field. Route choice and explosive barrels matter.
- **Cache Room:** A coolant recovery zone and a recovered defense turret create
  a temporary defensive stronghold.
- **Breaker Vault:** Two shock fields and reinforced barricades create limited
  safe lanes during the boss encounter.

## Visual Language

- Orange-red pulsing circles are dangerous shock fields.
- Cyan pools are beneficial to the player but slow enemies.
- Rotating colored diamonds are collectible skill cores.
- Green-cyan diamonds are recovered friendly turrets.
